Bethlehem University Welcomes New Students with Orientation Days



Bethlehem University has concluded its preparations for the new academic year 2024-2025 with a two-day orientation program for its incoming students. The orientation days, organized by the Deanship of Students Department, took place on Tuesday, 3 September, and Wednesday, 4 September 2024.
